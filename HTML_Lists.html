How to make areopress coffee

  1. Heat water to 200F
  2. Grind coffee fine
  3. Put paper filter in cap, rinse with water
  4. Put plunger into bottom of chamber, invert
  5. Add ground coffee to chamber
  6. Place over preheated mug
  7. Add water to chamber, stir
  8. Let brew for 1 minute
  9. Slowly press plunger down, approx 30 sec
  10. Add more hot water to taste
  11. Enjoy!
